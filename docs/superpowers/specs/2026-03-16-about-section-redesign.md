# About Section Redesign - Dragon City Mall

## Overview
Complete redesign of the About Us section to match a luxurious, premium aesthetic with elegant typography and side-by-side layout.

## Design Structure

### Layout
- **Left Side (50%)**: Text content and CTA
- **Right Side (50%)**: Large architectural image of Dragon City Mall

### Left Side Content
- **Label**: "WELCOME TO DRAGON CITY" (colored text, all caps, small)
- **Heading**: Bold serif typeface - "Its Time For Premium Shopping & Experiences"
- **Body Text**: Descriptive paragraph about Dragon City as the Kingdom's premier destination
- **CTA Button**: Gold/accent colored button - "Explore Now" or "Discover More"

### Right Side
- Large, high-quality image of Dragon City Mall exterior/architecture
- Modern, clean presentation

### Bottom Section - Statistics
4 stat cards in a row with icons:
1. **799+** Stores | "Retail & Wholesale"
2. **55K** Sqm | "Total Area"
3. **22,600+** Visitors | "Every Day"
4. **Est. 2015** | "A Decade of Excellence"

Each stat has:
- Icon representation
- Main number/stat
- Descriptive label

## Visual Style
- Luxurious & Premium feel
- Elegant, sophisticated
- Serif typography for headings
- Clean, minimal animations
- Gold/accent color for CTAs and highlights
- Light, neutral background

## Technical Details
- Responsive design (mobile: single column, desktop: side-by-side)
- Smooth scroll animations (subtle, not excessive)
- High-quality image optimization
- Modern React component using Tailwind CSS
